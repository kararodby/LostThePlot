Losing Growth, Finding the Plot: A Conversation About a Just Transition to a Smaller Global Economy
Thursday, July 23 · 5:30–7:00 PM  ·  In-Person (Chicago)
Co-Sponsor: Degrowth Institute
Register to attend →
Join Chicago's own Degrowth Institute (DGI) and Lost The Plot (LTP) for a short presentation and discussion on challenges to economic growth and circularity in our economy. DGI's Programs Coordinator, Jason Barahona Rosales, will give a short primer on degrowth, which calls for a just transition to a smaller economy. Then Madeleine Sullivan, a Digital Business Analyst at Caterpillar with deep expertise in circular economies, will give a brief history of how we became a single-use, disposable consumer product society, and discuss how our conversations about sustainability rarely attempt to actually challenge underserving status quo systems.

The most interesting Climate Week event you'll attend! Balance out your networking with some real discussion on what needs to be done to address our climate goals and meet folks working and fighting for real change.
